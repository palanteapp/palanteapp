# Palante Focus Group Questionnaire
**Format:** Digital Form (Google Forms) / Printed Handout
**Target Audience:** 20 Early Adopters

---

## **Part 1: The Experience (Page by Page)**
*Please rate and review specific areas of the app as you navigate through them.*

### **1. Welcome & Onboarding**
*(The very first impression, the "Welcome" modal, and the initial setup)*
*   **What stuck with you?** (What, if anything, created a "wow" moment?)
*   **What was confusing?** (Was there any point where you felt lost or bored?)
*   **Rating:** [1 - 5 Stars]

### **2. The Dashboard (Home Screen)**
*(The main hub where you see your day)*
*   **The "Vibe":** Does looking at this screen make you feel organized and calm, or overwhelmed?
*   **Clarity:** Is it obvious what you are supposed to click first?
*   **One Change:** If you could move, remove, or change one thing on this screen, what would it be?

### **3. The Stack (Routine Builder)**
*(Creating and managing your morning habits)*
*   **Ease of Use:** Did you understand how to build your stack?
    *   [ ] Yes, completely intuitive.
    *   [ ] Took a minute, but I got it.
    *   [ ] I struggled / needed help.
*   **The "Why":** Do you feel motivated to actually *do* these routines because of how they look/feel in the app?
*   **Missing:** What habit or step did you want to add but couldn't?

### **4. AI Coach**
*(The insights and daily guidance)*
*   **Relevance:** Did the advice feel personal to you, or generic?
*   **Voice:** How would you describe the Coach's personality? (e.g., "Helpful Friend", "Strict Teacher", "Robot")
*   **Value:** Would you open this every day?

### **5. Breathwork Station**
*(The breathing exercises and visualization)*
*   **Immersion:** Did the visuals help you focus on your breath?
*   **Friction:** Was anything distracting or hard to control?

### **6. The Sanctuary (Koi Pond & Soundscapes)**
*(Relaxation interactive elements)*
*   **Engagement:** Did you actually play with the fish/pond? For how long?
*   **Sound:** Did the soundscapes enhance your focus/relaxation, or were they just background noise?

## **Part 3: Group Discussion Prompts (For the Host)**
*These are open-ended questions to ask the room to spark debate and conversation.*

**1. The "Why Palante?" Question**
"There are a million habit trackers and meditation apps (Headspace, Calm, Notion). Why would you keep Palante on your home screen instead of those? What makes us different *to you*?"

**2. The Frequency Test**
"Be honest: Can you see yourself using this every single morning? If not, what is the specific barrier stopping you? (Time? Forgetfulness? Not enough value?)"

**3. Pricing & Value Perception**
"If this app was a paid subscription, what feature would make it 'worth it' for you? Only the visuals? The AI coaching? The privacy?"

**4. The "Vibe" Definition**
"We talk a lot about 'Vibe'. How would *you* describe the 'Vibe' of Palante to a friend who hasn't seen it yet?"
