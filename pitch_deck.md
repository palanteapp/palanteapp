## Page 1: The Vision
### **The Problem:** Fragmented Minds, Fragmented Apps
We live in an era of digital overwhelm. High-performers currently juggle 3-5 different apps to manage their day:
*   **Anxiety/Sleep:** Calm, Headspace
*   **Tasks:** Todoist, Things 3
*   **Habits:** Fabulous, Streaks
*   **Journaling:** Notion, Day One

**The Result:** Friction. Switching costs. A disconnect between *how we feel* and *what we need to do*. We are productive but burnt out, or mindful but directionless.

### **The Solution:** Palante
**Palante** (Spanish for *"Forward"*) is the first **Holistic Momentum Platform**. It unifies wellness and productivity into a single, beautiful flow. It doesn't just ask "What do you have to do?"; it asks, **"What type of motivation do you need today?"**

**Mission:** To help users move forward—not by grinding harder, but by aligning their energy with their actions.

## Page 2: The Product
### **Your Daily Companion**
Palante is built around a "Spa-Modern" aesthetic—clean, calming, and premium. It replaces chaos with clarity through four core pillars:

1.  **Vibe Check (The Differentiator):**
    *   **Muse** (Gentle): For recovery and flow.
    *   **Focus** (Balanced): For steady execution.
    *   **Fire** (Intense): For high-energy push.
    *   *The entire app interface, AI coaching tone, and content shift instantly to match this energy.*

2.  **Momentum & Accountability:**
    *   **Smart Nudges:** AI accountability partner that checks in based on your schedule.
    *   **Streak Recovery Economy:** A forgiving, gamified system where you use earned points to repair broken streaks, keeping you motivated.
    *   **Social Fire:** "One-tap" encouragement system to keep accountability partners connected with zero friction.

3.  **Wellness Suite:**
    *   **Soundscapes:** Binaural beats (Delta to Gamma) and ambient nature mixes.
    *   **Breathwork:** Haptic-guided exercises (4-7-8, Box, Coherent) for immediate state change.

4.  **Reflective Journaling:**
    *   **Smart Templates:** Context-aware prompts (Gratitude, Evening Review, Intentions) that cure writer's block instantly.
    *   **Morning Priming:** Intention setting to start the day.
    *   **Evening Check-In:** Close loops, celebrate wins, and track growth.
