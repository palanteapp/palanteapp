## 💰 Pricing Strategy

### **Free Tier: "Palante Core"**
**Goal:** Deliver real value, build habit, create desire for more

**What's Included:**
1. ✅ **Daily personalized quote/affirmation** (1 per day)
2. ✅ **Morning practice** (gratitude + intention setting)
3. ✅ **Basic breathwork** (3 techniques: Box, 4-7-8, Calm)
4. ✅ **Today's goals** (3 goals max)
5. ✅ **Basic progress tracking** (practices count)

**What's Limited:**
- ⏰ 1 quote per day (can't browse library)
- ⏰ 3 goals max (premium = unlimited)
- ⏰ Basic breathwork only (premium = 10+ techniques)
- ⏰ No AI coach
- ⏰ No journal insights
- ⏰ No accountability partner
- ⏰ No routine builder

**Why This Works:**
- Users get **real value** (not a trial)
- They build a **daily habit** (morning practice)
- They **want more** (AI coach, insights, routines)

---

### **Premium Tier: "Palante Plus"**
**Price:** $9.99/month or $79.99/year (33% discount)

**What's Unlocked:**

#### **1. AI Coach (Your Unfair Advantage)**
- ✅ Unlimited AI conversations
- ✅ Personalized guidance based on your goals
- ✅ Mood-aware responses (adapts to your energy)
- ✅ Proactive check-ins ("How's that project going?")

**Value:** This alone is worth $9.99/month (ChatGPT Plus is $20/month)

#### **2. Journal Insights (Your Unfair Advantage)**
- ✅ AI analyzes your reflections
- ✅ Identifies patterns ("You're stressed on Mondays")
- ✅ Suggests interventions ("Try breathwork before meetings")
- ✅ Weekly insights report

**Value:** Unique feature, no competitor has this

#### **3. Accountability Partner (Your Unfair Advantage)**
- ✅ Share goals with a friend/coach
- ✅ Weekly check-ins
- ✅ Mutual support
- ✅ Progress sharing

**Value:** Social accountability is proven to increase success rates by 65%

#### **4. Advanced Features**
- ✅ Unlimited quotes (browse full library)
- ✅ Unlimited goals
- ✅ Advanced breathwork (10+ techniques)
- ✅ Full meditation library (50+ sessions)
- ✅ Routine builder (custom morning/evening routines)
- ✅ Fasting timer
- ✅ Soundscapes
- ✅ Export data

#### **5. Premium Perks**
- ✅ Priority support
- ✅ Early access to new features
- ✅ No ads (if we add them later)
- ✅ Exclusive content

## 🎁 Free vs Premium: The Balance

### **The Golden Rule:**
**Free tier must be valuable enough to use daily, but limited enough to want premium.**

### **Free Tier Strategy:**

**What Free Users Get:**
- ✅ **Daily habit** (morning practice)
- ✅ **Real value** (personalized quote)
- ✅ **Basic tools** (breathwork, goals)
- ✅ **Progress tracking** (practices count)

**What Free Users Want:**
- 🔒 More quotes (browse library)
- 🔒 AI coach (personalized guidance)
- 🔒 Journal insights (pattern recognition)
- 🔒 Accountability partner (social support)
- 🔒 Advanced features (routines, meditation)

**The Trigger:**
After 7 days of daily use, show:
```
"You've completed 7 practices! 🎉
Want to go deeper?

Unlock:
• AI Coach for personalized guidance
• Journal Insights to track your growth
• Accountability Partner for support

Try Palante Plus free for 7 days"
```

## 💳 Payment & Conversion Strategy

### **1. Free Trial (7 Days)**
- All premium features unlocked
- No credit card required
- After 7 days, prompt to subscribe
- **Conversion rate:** 15-20% (industry standard)

### **2. Onboarding Optimization**
- Don't mention premium during onboarding
- Let users fall in love with free tier first
- After 3-7 days, introduce premium

### **3. In-App Prompts (Non-Intrusive)**
- "Want AI guidance? Upgrade to Plus"
- "Unlock journal insights with Plus"
- "Get accountability partner with Plus"

**Frequency:** Max 1 prompt per day, only when relevant

### **4. Annual Discount**
- $79.99/year (33% off monthly)
- Offer during checkout
- "Save $40 with annual plan"

### **5. Referral Program**
- "Invite a friend, both get 1 month free"
- Viral growth + retention boost
